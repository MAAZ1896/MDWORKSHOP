# Validate Author Using SGX
XXX SGX displays author information of titles published by the Author. Click **Validate** on the left pane. A confirmation message appears. Click **Yes**.
## Steps to validate author
1. Click **About the author** to open author bio.
2. Check author details against the information in the vendor central repository.
3. Click **Cover Image** and select author name.
4. Review the author’s website and verify the information with the repository.
5. Click **Design** to check the fonts, style and images used in previously published books.
6. Click **Copyright** on the right pane next to the Search bar.
7. Verify the copyrights from the list of last three books. If copyrights are updated flag it by clicking **Invalid** if not click **Valid**.
8. Click **Vendor central** on the left pane.
9. You are directed to marketing website, check whether the author has posted about the book release with updated cover image.
